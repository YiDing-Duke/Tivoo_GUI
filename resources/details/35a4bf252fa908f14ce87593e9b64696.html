Wellness Wednesday: Substance Abuse: Recognizing the Signs and Resources for Help

See description

Start Time: Wed Sep 28 16:30:00 EDT 2011

End Time: Wed Sep 28 17:30:00 EDT 2011

Description: Liz Prince, the assistant director of the Duke Wellness Center, will talk with students about recognizing the signs and symptoms of substance abuse, including how students can take steps to get help and what resources are available. This talk is a part of the ongoing Wellness Wednesdays series. Lunch will be provided. For more information, please contact Maria Magher at magher@law.duke.edu.
