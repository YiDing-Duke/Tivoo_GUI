Start Time: Thu Sep 29 20:00:00 EDT 2011

Description: All social science students and professionals are welcome to this OECD-led iLibrary training session. The OECD iLibrary contains over 5000 e-books, 5000 Excel tables, 400 datasets, and 2500 working papers. This data, vital to any honors thesis or research project, covers international measures of agriculture and trade, social issues like migration and health, and urban and rural development indicators. Don't let this valuable resource go unused!
