Open Studio with the Arts Theme House

None

Start Time: Fri Sep 30 19:30:00 EDT 2011

End Time: Fri Sep 30 23:30:00 EDT 2011

Description: Join the Arts Theme House for an Open Studio, and get those creative juices a-flowin'. All supplies are provided and include acrylics, watercolors, oil pastel, charcoal, ink, pencil, origami paper, and collage supplies. You're welcome to submit what you create for display at the Duke Arts Festival or in the Arts Theme House gallery space. Food is provided! The event will be held on the Main Quad in between Kilgo and Craven (the spot where benches are burned!). In the case of rain, the event will be in Kilgo O commons.
