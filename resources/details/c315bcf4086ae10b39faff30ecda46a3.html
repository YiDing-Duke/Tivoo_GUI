Doxita 4: Short Documentaries on Tour

CDS Front Desk

Start Time: Fri Sep 30 23:00:00 EDT 2011

End Time: Sat Oct 01 01:00:00 EDT 2011

Description: This is the fourth season for Doxita, a traveling festival of documentary films that are under 40 minutes in length. The program represents a wide variety of documentary (domestic and foreign, super-short and longer format, serious and funny), and is designed to profile the great content and artistic vision that nonfiction short films provide, but that people don¿t often get a chance to see.  READ MORE: http://www.doxita.org/
