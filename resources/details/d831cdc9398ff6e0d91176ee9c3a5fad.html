Guest Recital: Shuann Chai, pianist

Thompson, Elizabeth

Start Time: Sat Oct 01 00:00:00 EDT 2011

End Time: Sat Oct 01 01:30:00 EDT 2011

Description: Works by Mozart, Beethoven, Bach and Liszt. Chinese/American pianist Shuann Chai is an active and engaging performer, critically acclaimed for her interpretations on both modern and historical instruments. Recent projects include solo and chamber music programs at the Grachtenfestival Amsterdam (NL), the Frederick Collection (USA), St. Cecilia's Hall in Edinburgh (UK), the Oslo Opera House Chamber Music Series (Norway), and the Cobbe Collection at Hatchlands Park (UK), where she presented a pair of concerts on Chopin's own Pleyel grand piano. Shuann completed undergraduate degrees in Piano and Biology at Oberlin College and earned a Master's degree at the New England Conservatory in Boston.
